Change Your Habits

By changing habits, one can keep oneself away from disease and idleness. So exercise should be common for all.

Push up work

Push up work helps one to do chest, shoulders, arms, back, stomach and hips strong. It also improves one cardiovascular health, bone health and removes mental toughness.

Upward Salute

This exercise helps one to improve posture, stretch the body, enhance breathing, relieves stress, energize the body, Strengthen muscles and improve digestion.

One-Arm Dumbbell Row

It ensures one's upper back strength, core stability, improved posture, balance muscles development, joint health and engagement of multiple muscles groups.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl

It makes you improve Bicep Strength and Size, Improved Arm Definition, Functional Strength, Muscle Endurance and Versatility.

Run an Extra Mile easily

To run an extra mile easily, one needs to prepare oneself gradually. One should improve strength training, proper nutrition and ensure rest and recovery.
